Next BAPA Meeting - Tuesday, March 8th, 2016 in Milpitas - 6:30pm

Hello BAPA Members and Bay Area Pilots,

This is a reminder of our upcoming BAPA bi-monthly meeting next Tuesday, March 8th, 2016 at Red Lobster in Milpitas. Please note that this meeting will be on a slightly different day and week of our normal schedule.

I know a lot of pilots have difficulty getting to BAPA meetings due to traffic after work so the official meeting will not start until 7:30pm. We will have the private room at Red Lobster at 6:30pm, so please feel free to come early and socialize and eat/drink prior to the meeting. BAPA meetings are a great opportunity to mingle with local pilots. If you are not an official BAPA member (or even yet a paraglider) you are always welcome to come by and see what we are all about.

Are you interested in flying cross-country? Have you been flying for a while and want to progress to the next level but don't know how to get there? Are you ready to explore the skies and lose sight of the LZ? Or are you a new pilot to the area and just want to join up with other XC-minded folks? If your answer is yes to any of these questions you will definitely want to attend this meeting. NorCal's world-famous Jug Aggarwal (mentioned in this year's Cross Country Magazine's Travel Guide!) will be here to discuss the upcoming Sprint and XC Leagues for 2016. Bring all of your questions to Jug!

Please come and also spread the word, it should be a fantastic meeting. We will try to also have an A/V setup similar to the last meeting so that people can attend remotely. Details on the A/V web links to come later.

Details are as follows:

  • 6:30pm - Social Hour begins
  • 7:30pm - Official Meeting begins
